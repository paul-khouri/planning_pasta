Brief

I have been asked to develop a website for a new Pasta Bar called 1789, located in central Wellington
Mike the owner, has approached me, and we have discussed the key aims for the website.
The aims have been listed out in order of priority and this means that I can complete and have feedback on tasks at regular intervals.
Before starting the website I completed some UX studies

  • Looking at the types of users who will be accessing the site and the kind of design and experiences that they expect when they are online
  • Doing some competition analysis to get a sense of the design style and features of websites that perform similar functions.
  • Completing a basic plan for the site, with the understanding that this most likely will evolve over the project

Sprint One

Aim: To get a home page design sorted out. This includes trialling some different visuals and getting basic introductory content in

Repository link

This sprint was really lots of trialling and testing and get feedback from Mike

Reflection

This was the first design and I chose an Italian theme. It was really helpful to assemble the page and get used to working with Boostrap 5. I explored how well the site resized on different screens.
This was all working 'okay' and I spent quite a bit of time exploring features such as media queries and using the root style to set colour variables. I also spent a lot of time trying to get the black line framing the text.
